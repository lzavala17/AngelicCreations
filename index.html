ABOUT US

We are a small family operated business since 2011. We take a great deal of pride in our work. We believe that everyone should be able to afford beautiful flowers on a tight budget. We are dedicated to our customers and do our very best to fulfill your every floral needs. I also am available as your personal assistant for your special days. Not only take care of your floral arrangement needs but to make sure all the little things get taken care of too. There are lots of details that can easily get overlooked and part of my job is to make sure you have a plan and we stick to your plan as much as possible. Every event is different and my goal is to help envision what you are looking to accomplish and make it happen, at a reasonable cost. Let me help you, I'm just a phone call away.
